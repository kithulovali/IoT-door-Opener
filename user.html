Face Recognition Access

  1. Click "Start Camera" to activate face recognition
  2. Position your face clearly in the camera view
  3. Click "Capture & Access" to analyze your face
  4. Wait for the system to verify your identity
  5. If successful, the door will unlock automatically
Tips for better recognition:
  • Ensure good lighting on your face
  • Look directly at the camera
  • Remove sunglasses or face coverings
  • Keep a neutral expression
  • Make sure your face is centered in the frame
